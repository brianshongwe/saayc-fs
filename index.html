About us

Our History

The Southern African Association of Youth Clubs(SAAYC)is a registered youth development organisation, governed by an independent board that is manged and coordinated by a team of youth practitioners , led by the Executive director. SAAYC provides training , mentorship and program suppor to youth organisation,youth leaders ,individuals, young professionals and youth workers.

Based in South Africa , the organisation has a national footprint, with its head-office in Gauteng(Randburg),and physical youth centers in Gauteng(Soweto,Ekurhuleni Municipality and orange otange farm) and the free State (QwaQWa),with a growing presence into Southern Africa.

Established in 1937,SAAYC is a legacy brand with decades of experience and a rich History of positive change when it comes to the youth demographic in Southern Africa. We are trusted name within the industry, and we have well established relationships with other organisations and partners who share our vision in contributing to positive socio-economic transformation through supporting the youth in Southern Africa. A key factor that sets us appart is the approach of allowing young people to determine the interventions, and the results.

OUR MISSION

"To facilitate and implement intergrated youth development programmes through collaboration with strategic partners for the benefit of our members and young people in Southern Africa"

OUR GOALS

SAAYC exists to promote positive transformation of youth in Southern Africa in a manner that facilitates the changing of the socio-economic environment for a better future for society as a whole.

We work closely with local youth organisaions and youth community leaders to influence positive thinking, provide opportunities to young people, educate the youth when it comes to both social and ptractic al skills; and help youngb people to challenge the odds.

By motivating, supporting and inspiring leadership behaviour in young people, SAAYC aims to develop the leaders and change-makers of tomorrow who will pay it forward and help uplift their peers and communities.

Our services

ICTAGE PROGRAMME

Southern African Association of Youth Clubs(SAAYC)Implements a comprehensive computer literacy programme aimed at giving people skills to use computer technology in everyday life to develop new social and economic opportunities for themselves, their families,and their communities.

I.T ESSENTIALS

IT Essentials covers fundamental computer and career skills for entry-level IT jobs to develop critical thinking and problem solving skills using both real equipment and Cisco Packet Tracer , a network configuration simultation tool.

Introduction to cybersecurity

The introduction to cybersecurity course explores the broad topic of cybersecurity in a way that matters to you . Learn how to protect your personal data and privacy online and in social media , and and why more and more I.T jobs require cybersecurity awareness.

Introduction to IoT

The introduction to IoT course provides an overview of key concepts and challenges related to digital transformation . The course examines the evolution of the internet and how the interconnection of people , processes and how data transforms.

LIFE SKILLS Programme

The SAAYC launch Programme is primarily developed to anchor beneficiaries from centres to br fully capacitated in financial literacy, interpersonal skills; Civic Engagement(Active citizenship) and personal development it addresses the needs of teenagers and young adults and youth around the SAAYC centres who seek to learn basic essential life skills .

EDUCATIONAL SUPPORT PROGRAMME

Complements and builds on the efforts done by the department of education in assisting Grade 9 learners in choosing subjects in line with their dream career and improving their literacy rate at a critical stage in their life.

HOLIDAY PROGRAMME

To provide a fun and safe environment where young people learn new skills, through activities that stimulates critical thinking.
